About
Pollux 2023

Pollux is a 48 hour hackathon with endless creativity. Students are challenged to explore what they know by creating projects following their passions. Spending time on projects outside the classroom is the best way to not only learn what you love, but also find new interests. You are going to mix tech and innovation to build up a mixture of products that is beneficial and enhances the lifestyle of people. In a universe of infinite possibilities, you have 48 hours to innovate and show us what you got!
